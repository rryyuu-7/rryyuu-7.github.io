About Me

I’m a third-year Mechanical Engineering undergraduate at IIT Delhi with a strong interest in intelligent energy and dynamical systems. My work spans electrification technologies, battery and EV systems, hydrogen-based energy pathways, and advanced control for complex mechanical systems. I’m particularly interested in how physical modeling, optimization, and data-driven methods can be combined to design efficient, robust, and scalable energy solutions. I enjoy working from first principles — understanding the physics deeply, and then building computational or control frameworks on top of it. Long-term, I aim to contribute to high-impact energy and autonomous system technologies through research or advanced engineering roles.

Interests

  • AI-driven Digital Twins for Energy and Mechanical Systems
  • Optimization and Energy System Design
  • Design and manufacture of EV powertrains, with precise calculations
  • System-Level Modeling and Control of Dynamical Systems
  • Optimizing cooling systems, and working on Heat and Mass Transfer problems
